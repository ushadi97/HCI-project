Members of the Council


The council is the supreme governing body, and is vested with the administration, management and control of the affairs of the institute The Academic Affairs Board, functioning under the Council, decides on standards, contents, curricula and evaluation process of academic programs undertaken by the institute. The Director General, the chief executive officer, is directly responsible to the council for the efficient functioning of the institute. The Chief Accounting Officer is the secretory of the Ministry of Education
